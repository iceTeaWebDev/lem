Introducing LEM House Production

Your POD solution for unique, high-quality products on diverse e-commerce platforms.

PARTNERSHIP INVITATION

LEM Production House, established in June 2023, operates in the rapidly growing field of design for printed products. With a focus on Etsy, the e-commerce platform, LEM has gained consumer recognition, achieving stable revenue and positive growth. The company aims to serve and satisfy global customers through continuous effort, innovation, and collaboration, particularly targeting the preferences of consumers in the United States and Europe, where online shopping for uniquely designed products is prevalent.

01.

Unveiling Our Vision for the Future

Becoming a leading design company, pioneering innovation and revolutionizing the Print on Demand (POD) industry. With relentless dedication and passion from every team member, we aim to create groundbreaking, impressive designs for export to major countries such as the United States and Europe. Meeting the essential needs of customers in large nations, we strive to establish ourselves as a forefront innovator in the POD sector.

03.

Core Values

Professionalism: With a vision to become a pioneering designer, leading in the POD (Print on Demand) sector, LEM's top priority is product quality and customer experience. By continuously updating customer preferences, fostering creativity, innovating designs, and enhancing customer service, LEM aims to meet market demands and strive for perfection every day.

Dedication: At LEM, the customer is at the center (Customer-Centric). Every product designed at LEM is aimed at delivering satisfaction by actively listening, observing, and understanding customer desires, promptly addressing their needs. Each product delivered to customers is meticulously cared for by LEM, ensuring satisfaction for every customer.

Innovation: Innovation is an essential trend in every aspect of life, especially in the fashion industry. Recognizing this, LEM places a high value on continuous innovation and creativity in all activities, committing to deliver superior design quality and peace of mind for customers.

04.

Action strategy

Build a strong LEM brand, aiming to become a leading entity in Vietnam for the design and export of fashion and handmade products. Enhance service quality and leverage the company's human resources effectively.

CUSTOMIZE: Leading the way, our activities are always directed towards the target customer group, meeting market and industry preferences and trends.

PARTNERSHIP: Building an online store network with a focus on long-term strategic partnerships based on trust and kindness to bring sustainable benefits for both parties.

05.

Strategic products

Designing and manufacturing fashion products based on individual requests and societal trends. Designing and crafting handmade decorative items.

06.

Distribution channels and market

Selling online on the Etsy e-commerce platform. Marketing in the United States, Australia, Canada, and Europe.

Collaboration Content

Building a Store System: In order to expand business development, LEM and you will collaborate to establish a store on the Etsy e-commerce platform. You will be the named owner of the online shop within the brand-building and development initiative led by LEM.

To accomplish this, you will need to prepare

Computer Or Laptop

Computer with Windows, TeamViewer, or UltraView, and an internet connection. LEM suggests enhancing personal information security by setting passwords for crucial files.Using a dedicated computer for business purposes.

Etsy account

Registering an account on Etsy: LEM's IT team will assist you during the account creation process to avoid any errors. You will need to fill in personal information as required by Etsy, such as ID, PR, and bank account details.

Email And Phone Number

During business operations: As the store owner, you may receive emails or messages from Etsy's system or strangers. Stay calm and contact LEM's staff who are directly working with you for consultation on how to handle these communications.

LEM WILL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR WHAT TASKS?

  • Arranging personnel to accompany you throughout the business operation, starting from the registration of the store on the e-commerce platform.

  • Preparing a team to manage the operations of the stores.

  • Developing PR and marketing plans for promoting store business.

  • Collaborating with relevant parties in business production activities, such as printing, garment manufacturing, and shipping.

  • Settling and fulfilling financial obligations with store owners.

Collaboration Methods

Business Cooperation Contract (BCC) is a collaboration agreement signed between LEM and partners to engage in business cooperation, profit-sharing based on agreed terms without establishing an economic organization.

Adherence to agreed-upon financial rules.
